## 📖 关于本书 | About This Book

This work is submitted in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Doctor of Arts in Mathematical Sciences.

> 🇨🇳 本作品作为数学科学博士学位（Doctor of Arts）要求的部分成果提交。

This book's main goals are to show you what mathematical thinking, problem-solving, and proof-writing are really all about, to show you how to do those things, and to show you how much fun they really are!

> 🇨🇳 本书的主要目标是向你展示数学思维、问题解决和证明写作究竟是什么，教你如何做这些事情，并让你发现它们有多有趣！
